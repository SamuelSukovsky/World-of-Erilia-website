World of Erilia

is a modern fantasy setting designed by Samuel Sukovský as a foundation for stories experienced through the medium of Dungeons and Dragons, "a collaborative storytelling game where one person plays the narrator and supporting cast while everyone else plays a main character."

Immerse yourself in the intricate era of change and progress, in the world of nations and covert guilds, and in stories of heroes and monsters, either as an adventurer, or as the game master!

Change and Progress

The year is 2093 of the Laurian calendar and the prominent nations of Erilia have just recently entered the cybernetics era. Through perfecting global information networks and the development of advanced artificial intelligence, both cultural and technological development of the entire world started to accelerate exponentially.

These are the times of change, as the first thinking and feeling robots walk the streets of Yeong amongst all the other humanoids, and the laboratories of Simis corporation produce their first bio-engineered symbiotes.

These are the times of progress, as the auto-forges of Norforge Union mass produce fusion powered battle tanks, and Elenodor's first seventh generation fighters take to the skies.

Nations and Covert Guilds

Despite dangers to civilization being plentiful, the people remain divided, as they had been for thousands of years now. Be they global superpowers or small criminal gangs, differing factions play a role in all aspects of people's lives, and clash with one another on a daily basis.

This is the world of nations, where the Shogunate of Chalk prepares its armies for conflict with the neighbouring republic of Vico, and the United States of Oston raise their own to defend their protectorate.

This is the world of covert guilds, where the Tatorincse Shadow Guard slowly work their way to overthrow the corrupt politicians of Boule, and where said politicians use their immorally gained funds to further the goals of the Cult of Yaguxoth.

Heroes and Monsters

Even though the population of humanoid races in Erilia had exceeded the mind-bogglingly large number of a billion during the past century, traveling most of the planet's surface is still immensely dangerous for a regular person. With beasts, monstrosities, and rogue humanoid bands running rampant in the wilderness, towns and settlements are often forced to contract groups of adventurers to solve problems that local military and law enforcement aren’t able to deal with.

These are the stories of monsters, in which dire wolves, bandits, and dragons terrorize the peaceful folk of Erilia.

These are stories of you, adventurer. It is up to you, to step up and save the world.
