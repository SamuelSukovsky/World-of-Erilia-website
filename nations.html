Nations of Erilia

are plentiful and diverse, from the smallest merchant kingdoms, to the greatest supernations. With international relations sometimes being rather strenuous, and great rivalries abound, the continents are on the cusp of war almost at all times.

Lutena

is the eastmost part of the Taina-Lute-Zadzar continental landmass. It is the cradle of several human civilizations, and the most culturally diverse part of the world.

Most nations of Lutena have roots in empires of old, and despite their fall from glory, they all remember a golden age of their own with pride.
